About Bianca Roelandschap


My name is Bianca Roelandschap, I was born in 1975 in Utrecht, the Netherlands. I lived with my parents, older sister and brother in Nieuwegein.

I didn't have an easy childhood. My parents got devorced when I was 11.

Back in that time, it was unusual to have parents who got devorced, so I was bullied a lot at school. 

I was sexually abused right after my parents were devorced by a "friend of the family", who was kind to me. And made me feel like I was someone.

Why am I telling you this? Well my story is one of milions in this world, and instead of feeling sorry for myself, I made (and still am making) something of my life! Never the less, this should not happen to anyone (children or adults).

So, if I could change just one thing in this world, it would be the way people treat each other. If it's because your black, a woman, gay, or anything other than what people know and trust, there is no reason what so ever to abuse someone!

Like I said, i made something of my life. Sinds I was just a little girl, I wanted to be a mother when I grew up. So after Edwin and I got married in 1999, we tried to get pregnant.

Unfortunatly after being pregnant for 13 weeks, we got a miscarriage. We were devasstated.

I was scared to try again, but we did. Our son Nigel was born in 2003!

When I was giving birth, I had some complications. So I was brought to de OR. It was very scary, espescialy for Edwin, because he was left holding our son.

Well I'm still here, so I made it out. But I was told I could never have any children again without the change that things could go wrong, and I would die giving birth. So we decided not to have anymore children.

We still live in Nieuwegein, in the Netherlands.
