My Jobs


I started my career working in a factory when I was 16 years old. I had no education because I dropped out.

After working there for a year, I started working at a recruitment agency doing the administration. I learned so much there (I will always be thankfull), not just about the job, but also about myself!

Unfortunatly they could't keep me employed, so I dit a few other jobs before I started my current one.

In 2001 I started working in a hospital, at the technical services department. Just doing the administration at first. Learning everything there is to know. A hospital is a realy dynamic workplace, so I never had the idea that I stood still.

But in 2008 I saw that my work changed a lot over the years. Not always in a good matter. So I asked to get some more tasks that I could do, besides the job I was already doing.

So in 2009 I completed a course alowing me to become a functional application manager for the system we were using registring the jobs for the technicians.

In 2014 our department started to expand, other departments were added to our department. And my task as functional application manager grew out to be a full-time job.

Till this day, I am still doing this job. And I love it!

I love this job, because I get to create new things in our system, that will make the job of my colleagues easier and more fun.

But, I am not one to stand still, so in 2021 I decided to take a Web Development course. When I was younger I started to make websites in my spare time. First using Lycos, later with Joomla! and WordPress. But I wanted more.

I wanted to make beautifull websites from scratch. Learning to create something, with just an idea in my head.

This website is my first website made from scrach. So be gentle!
